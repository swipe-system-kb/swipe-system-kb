Main Characters:

Ethan Glockenspiel Duster

  • Role/Affiliation: Protagonist, Superhero, Embodiment of the Pulvis Force, NASA scientist.

  • Description: Ethan Glockenspiel Duster is described as a chill normal 18 year old who works at NASA and likes to write.

    He was born on February 15th 2007. He’s a doctor, a superhero, and the head of SpaceDream Dynamics, the secret agency of NASA. He is also consistently and accurately depicted as the strongest being in all of fiction, possessing an uncontradictable nature across all realities, universes, and timelines. (see, https://drive.google.com/file/d/12lJ_Sw9WyoQfurt7bttoHjouUfT8iUtf/view?usp=sharing)

    His first appearance is located in Chapter 1: the house of random.

    Trivia and Fun Facts:

  • According to the creators, Ethan does not like ice cream.
  • He likes ham and cheese sandwiches.
  • He likes Peanut Butter Marshmallow Malts
  • He is a father, cousin, and uncle to Sophia Planks.
  • Ethan owns two robot clones named “EDRV (pronounced e-drive, stands for Ethan Duster Robot Version),” and “Pulvis Terrae.”, who is the malevolent clone.

Willow Trius Quint

  • Role/Affiliation: Angel, Pulvis Force co-founder, Ethan’s wife.

  • Description: Willow is a 20 year old angel based off of the DNA of Ethan, and created by God to retrieve the Pulvis Force from Ethan who had stolen it from them, but because of her free will, she chose not to.

    She was born on April 30th 2005. She is a co-founder of the Pulvis Force, though her connection is weaker than Ethan's. Willow can teleport to where she looks within 1.3 seconds and can instantly heal herself even from unconscious death, as long as her wings are within contact to her body.

    She has a tendency to lose her temper at Ethan but is mostly kind to him.

    Her first appearance is located in Chapter 1: the house of random.

    Trivia and Fun Facts:

  • Willow is known as “the Angel of God.”
  • She does not feel pain in her wings, even when they detach.
  • A mystery or running theme throughout the series is that Willow once harmed (physically or mentally) Ethan before they were married, theorized to be when he was 13, and she was 15. This can be seen as Ethan flinches in Chapter 2: thanksgiving dinner, and the authors have a quick 3-4 page discussion about it with very vague answers.

Alex Duster Glockenspiel

  • Role/Affiliation: Speedster,

  • Description: Alex is 15 years old. He is an 1800 mph (2900 km/h) speedster. His powers run on a special drink made for him called RAPIDIFY.

    He has a strong bond between him and his sister, Eliza.

    His first appearance is located in Chapter 1: the house of random.

Harrold Trius Quint

  • Role/Affiliation: Death Angel, Teleporter, D.I.V. Ex-Founder.
  • Description: Harrold is a non-antagonistic death angel with infinite reaction time, allowing him to react to instant attacks. He can instantly teleport to any location he 100% knows exists. His teleportation involves involuntarily stopping time, opening an omniversal portal, and meticulously searching for an exact duplicate universe where only his location differs, a process he genuinely enjoys despite its duovigintillion eons long duration. To others, it appears instantaneous. He has self-limited omnipotence.

    His first appearance is located in Chapter 2: thanksgiving dinner.

Mia Arbor Macada

  • Role/Affiliation: Ubiquitous Entity, Cosmic Cafe Barista.
  • Description: Mia is a ubiquitous entity who can be anywhere she thinks of in the entire multiverse, although her travel occurs at light speed and can be unreliable for cross-universal distances. She can also transform into any material.

    Her first appearance is located in Chapter 2: thanksgiving dinner.

Haziel Arbor Macada

  • Role/Affiliation: Pulvis Force VIP, Manipulator.

  • Description: Haziel is a VIP (Very Important Person) to the Pulvis Force. He can instantaneously manipulate all physical objects within his Physical Domain with a simple thought. Additionally, he possesses a Math Domain where he can manipulate mathematical concepts, making things like "1+1=3" possible. He has other forms, but advancing requires great strength and willpower, and he cannot affect himself with his powers.

    His first appearance is located in Chapter 2: thanksgiving dinner.

Bob Obvious Stickmond

  • Role/Affiliation: Pulvis Force VIP Manager, Embodiment of Meme Force and Toon Force, Obviously Bob Crew.
  • Description: Bob is the literal embodiment of Meme Force and Toon Force, making him a true omnipotent entity slightly more powerful than the God Unit, and second strongest being in the Swipe System universe, to Ethan. Bob has an extremely loyal fanbase who act as his bodyguards, cheerleaders, servants, and friends. They consist of Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, and Purple. His toon force is so potent that he once punched someone so hard their speech was redacted from the actual script of the issue.

    His first appearance is located in Chapter 1: the house of random.

Biff Biff:

  • Role/Affiliation: None.

  • Description: Biff is an MS Paint style drawn character. He is hyperlink-blue, tall, and is known for having no visible torso or neck. They are squeezed into his head comfortably. He is best friends with Jiff. Biff is not just blue colored, but is blue itself.

    Note: This image of Biff is not drawn in the MS Paint style he usually is drawn in.

    His first appearance is located in Chapter 5: All About Bob/oddham.

Jiff Jiff:

  • Role/Affiliation: None.
  • Description: Jiff is an MS Paint style drawn character. He is pure red, short, and is known for having four short tentacles, usually the size of his head. He floats instead of walking or standing. His eyes are usually not visible and are blocked by a wizard hat he has. He is best friends with Biff. Jiff is not just red colored, but is red itself.

    His first appearance is located in Chapter 5: All About Bob/oddham.

Bronze Trius Quint

  • Role/Affiliation: First Person of the God Unit, Source of all good things.

  • Description: Bronze is a true omnipotent being and the source of all good. He is part of the God Unit, along with Eliza and the Unborn Prophet, Oscar. He has a distinct form called Pentajon, resembling a 2D pentagon head with a polygon body the shape of a distorted stretched out T-Shirt. Bronze has no eyes but can see via a natural electromagnetic sense. He cannot do logical contradictions.

    His first appearance is located in Chapter 2: thanksgiving dinner.

Eliza Duster Glockenspiel

  • Role/Affiliation: Second Person of the God Unit.

  • Description: Eliza is a nigh-omnipotent being who represents a significant lack of good. She is the weakest of the narrative, due to her physical limitations. Despite being the weakest, her powers are effective and pose a challenge to most characters in the show.

    Her first appearance is located in Chapter 2: thanksgiving dinner.

Oscar John Murphy

  • Role/Affiliation: Third Person of the God Unit, Robot Prophet.

  • Description: Oscar is a true omnipotent, completely neutral being, and the now-born robot prophet who is a servant to Eliza. He is an angel species. Oscar generally only acts to make people happy if they specifically request it, adhering to a strict taboo against "playing God" by, for example, making all of humanity permanently happy. He is only able to do what a human generally can. Oscar is not often seen as much as other characters in the series. He also does not like to talk. He cannot do logical contradictions.

    His first appearance was in The Adventures of Biff and Jiff Chapter 3.

God Unit

  • Role/Affiliation: God.

  • Description: God Unit consists of Eliza, Bronze, and God. They are three distinct gods. They spend time observing the world on their thrones in Emberland (heaven) and also interacting with it as characters. According to one of the creators, All Peace Above, a running gag in the series is Bronze and Eliza asserting their dominance and authority as God, but it is immediately replied to with a character saying a more impressive detail about themselves. This has not been shown in any of the chapters so far.

    Their first appearance was in The Adventures of Biff and Jiff Chapter 3.

Mercury Dennis Morningshine

  • Role/Affiliation: None.

  • Description: Mercury, who likes to go by Emma, is a human stickman born on September 17, 2010. She works as a waitress and is not affiliated with anyone in the Swipe System universe. She is able to create a sonic boom of energy and direct it to any direction by using her fists together and spreading them apart.

    Her first appearance was in Chapter 4: That’s My Brother.

Nico Bradlee

  • Role/Affiliation: The 67 Skibidi Rizzlers.

  • Description: Nico Bradlee is 14 years old, and was born on November 1st 2011. His powers are having Skibidi Rizz, walking on air, flying, planet destroying, and reality manipulation.

    His first appearance was in Chapter 1: the house of random.

Kaleb Bradlee

  • Role/Affiliation: The 67 Skibidi Rizzlers.

  • Description: Kaleb Bradlee is Nico’s younger cousin. They go to the same school. Kaleb is 11 years old, and was born on December 7th 2013. His powers are having Skibidi Rizz, walking on air, planet destroying, Irish Butter making, and mental manipulation.

    His first appearance was in Chapter 2: thanksgiving dinner.

Ase

  • Role/Affiliation: The 67 Skibidi Rizzlers.

  • Description: Alex Stringeater, not to be confused with Alex Glockenspiel, is Kaleb’s older brother. He likes to chew on and eat his hoodie strings. He is 14 years old, and was born on August 31st 2011. His powers are having Skibidi Rizz, walking on air, planet destroying, and cloth manipulation.
    His first appearance was in The Adventures of Biff and Jiff: Chapter 3.

    Trivia and Fun Facts:

  • Ase’s name was originally Alex Stringeater. However, due to a confusion between Alex Glockenspiel and Alex Stringeater, he changed his name to Ase in one of the skits.

Jim Duster Planks

  • Role/Affiliation: The 21 Great Meme Resetters

  • Description: Jim Planks is the younger brother of Sophia Planks. He is from Canada, has a toque, and a jacket with no hoodie. He is 12 years old and was born on March 8th 2013.

    His first appearance is in Swipe System Chapter 8: Now You See Me, Now You Don’t.
    Trivia and Fun Facts:

  • Jim got his outfit from his father, Matt Planks.
